Health and Safety

Headache

Many hours on screen, head say “too much!” Maybe light problem, maybe stress. Best way is sit good, not too close, drink water, relax brain.

Learn more about headaches on NHS

Eye Strain

Look at screen long time, eyes feel bad. Too much light or no blinking enough make it worse. Must take break, look far, blink more, maybe use screen glasses.

Backache

Sit too much, back not happy. Bad chair or wrong sit make it worse. Need sit straight, use chair that help back, stand sometimes, move body little.

RSI

Typing, mouse too much, hand start hurt. Same move many time is not good. Need rest hands, not press hard, use support under wrist, do little stretch.

RBCR

Sit long time, legs no move, blood slow down. Can make big problem. Best way is stand sometimes, move legs, drink water enough.
